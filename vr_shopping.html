VR Shopping

The VR Shopping project is an immersive virtual reality application that allows users to explore and shop in a fully interactive 3D environment, simulating a realistic retail experience.

It leverages cutting-edge technology to enhance user engagement and streamline the online shopping process, providing a unique and enjoyable alternative to traditional e-commerce platforms.

Technologies Used: Unity, C#, VR simulator, 3D Modeling
