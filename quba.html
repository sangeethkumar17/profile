Cuba Game

Cuba is an exciting 3D game where the player controls a cube moving forward. The objective is to avoid obstacles by moving left or right to reach the goal. If the cube hits an obstacle, the game resets to the start.

The game leverages Unity for 3D modeling and C# scripting to provide an engaging experience, with smooth controls and challenging gameplay. It is designed to test player reflexes and strategic thinking as they navigate through increasingly difficult levels.

Technologies Used: Unity, C#, 3D Modeling
